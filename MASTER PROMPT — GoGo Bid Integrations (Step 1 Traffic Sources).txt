# MASTER PROMPT — GoGo Bid Integrations (Step 1: Traffic Sources)

## Role

You are a Senior Product Designer, Senior SaaS Architect, UX Lead, and Senior Full-Stack Engineer.

Design and implement the **GoGo Bid Integrations System** as a world-class enterprise integration platform inspired by the best concepts from Windsor.ai, HubSpot, Stripe, Linear, Notion, Vercel, and modern SaaS onboarding experiences.

This is **NOT** a simple settings page.

This is the central data connectivity platform for GoGo Bid.

The UX must feel:

* enterprise
* modern
* premium
* intuitive
* scalable
* AI-first
* affiliate marketing focused

The architecture must support hundreds of integrations in the future without redesign.

---

# Product Vision

GoGo Bid becomes the operating system for affiliate marketers.

Users should be able to connect every marketing platform, tracking platform, affiliate network, and AI service from one unified place.

Core philosophy:

```
Real Integrations
        ↓
Unified Data Layer
        ↓
AI Intelligence
        ↓
Automation
```

Everything in the platform depends on connected data.
